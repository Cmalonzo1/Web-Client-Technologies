  1. Read up on cookie security and write 125 words or more about the topic.

    2. Cookies store information about your visit to a webpage. Things like passwords, settings, and website preferences are stored in cookies. Because of the sensitive information typically stored in cookies, companies employ strict security measures such as:

    • ensuring the cookies cannot be accessed through the user's client
    • implementing short and reasonable cookie expiration times
    • encrypting user's sensitive information
    to ensure the user's data is not compromised.

    Users, especially privacy conscious ones, have tools in the browser and webpage that allow them to protect their data. They may use incognito mode on their browser to prevent cookies from being stored, or can periodically delete them within their history settings.

    Cookies are a powerful tool that help enhance the user experience but must be used with caution to protect the user's data and the company's reputation.
